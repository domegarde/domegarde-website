Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Domegarde ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects personal data in connection with the Domegarde platform, in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and French data protection law.

1. Who We Are

Domegarde is the data controller for personal data processed through our marketing website, and acts as a data processor on behalf of landlords for tenant and property data managed within the app. Domegarde is based in Paris, France.

2. What Data We Collect

CategoryExamples
Account dataName, email address, phone number, password (encrypted)
Property dataProperty address, rental type, lease dates, compliance records
Financial dataRent and charge records, deposit records
DocumentsLeases, certificates, inventories, and other files uploaded to the platform
Maintenance recordsIssue descriptions and status updates
Contact form dataName, email, phone, message, preferred contact language, for enquiries submitted via our website

3. Why We Process This Data

  • Performance of a contract: to provide the Domegarde service to landlords and their tenants.
  • Legitimate interest: to respond to enquiries, improve the service, and maintain security.
  • Legal obligation: where retention or disclosure is required by law.

4. Who We Share Data With

We use a small number of subprocessors to operate the service:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage, hosted in the EU (Paris region).
  • Vercel — hosting for our website and application.

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

5. International Data Transfers

Where a subprocessor is based outside the European Economic Area, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses to ensure your data remains protected.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as your account remains active, and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes. Landlords can request deletion of tenant data once a tenancy has ended, subject to any legal retention requirements (e.g. for tax or dispute purposes).

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Request a portable copy of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Lodge a complaint with the French data protection authority (CNIL)

To exercise these rights, contact us via our contact form.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit, database-level access controls (Row Level Security) that restrict each user to their own data, and restricted access to production systems.

9. Cookies

Our website and app use only the essential cookies required to keep you securely logged in. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

10. Children

Domegarde is not directed at, or intended for use by, children, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to registered users.

12. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled can be sent via our contact form.
