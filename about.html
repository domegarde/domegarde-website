Why Domegarde exists

We believe every landlord deserves to feel protected.

Not just insured. Protected — from missed deadlines, scattered paperwork, and the quiet stress of not being entirely sure everything's in order. That's the job Domegarde was built to do.

The problem

Three ways property admin quietly breaks down

01

Compliance slips through the cracks

DPE certificates, insurance renewals, safety diagnostics — each with its own deadline, none of them tracked anywhere but a landlord's memory or a spreadsheet nobody opens until it's too late.

02

Documents live everywhere and nowhere

Leases, inventories, certificates, and receipts end up spread across email threads, downloads folders, and phone photos — findable only if you remember exactly where you put them.

03

Managing property still feels like a second job

Independent landlords handle accounting, compliance, and tenant relations manually, using tools built for enterprise portfolios or nothing built for the job at all.

Where we're headed

From France to Europe. From MVP to AI.

Domegarde starts as structured, dependable property administration for landlords in France. The long-term shape of the platform is an agentic AI steward — a layer that doesn't just store your compliance data but actively watches it, flags what needs attention, and eventually acts on your behalf. We're building the foundation for that now.
